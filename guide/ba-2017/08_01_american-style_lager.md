- **Clarity:** Chill haze should not be present
- **Perceived Malt Aroma & Flavor:** Malt sweetness is very low to low
- **Perceived Hop Aroma & Flavor:** Not perceived to very low
- **Perceived bitterness:** Not perceived to very low

## Vital Statistics:

- OG: 1.040 - 1.048
