Beer Style Guidelines - Privacy Policy

This is our Privacy Policy for Beer Style Guidelines. We do our best to limit any information we transmit using our applications. In fact, the only data that we transmit is analytical and crash data.

As of Beer Style Guidelines v2019.1, this data is collected via Apple's App Analytics, and can be disabled at any time using these instructions.

As of Beer Style Guidelines v2022.1, data is also collected via Telemetry Deck. this data is all anonymized and only includes "page views" and anonymous actions.

As of Beer Style Guidelines v2024.5, data is only collected via Apple's App Analytics. Telemetry Deck has been removed from the app.

We will never sell your info. Ever.

Your Consent

By using our apps, you consent to our Privacy Policy.

Changes to our Privacy Policy

If we decide to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this page.
